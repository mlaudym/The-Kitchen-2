Sriracha Mac & Cheese

Full Recipe: Sriracha Mac & Cheese

Cook Time: 1 Hour

Prep Time: 30 Minutes

Ingredients

1 lb. Sriracha Pasta

1 lb. White Cheddar Cheese

3 Leaves of Kale



1 lb. Thick-Cut Bacon

3 cups of Whole Milk

Salt & Pepper

  1. Wash the kale thoroughly, then dice it into one-inch strips. On a greased sheet pan, put the kale in the oven at 375 degrees for 30 minutes or until crisp.

  2. Fill a large pot of pasta with heavily salted water. Bring to a boil. Add the pasta and let boil for 10 minutes or until al dente.

  3. While the pasta boils, dice bacon into 1" strips and cook in frying pan on stove top over medium-high heat.

  4. In a separate pot, combine cheese, milk, salt, and pepper. Over low heat, bring toa simmer, occationally stirring.

  5. Drain Pasta and combine the pasta with the cheese sauce in the warm pot.

  6. Portion Mac and Cheese into bowls and garnish heavily with crispy kale and bacon.

Enjoy!
